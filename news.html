What's New

With the Season 1 update having just been released, we thought it would be appropriate to share with you some of the changes that were made. The most important change was the addition of three guns, the M13b Assault Rifle, the Bas-P SMG, better known as the AAC Honey Badger in real life, and the Victus XMR Sniper Rifle. Whether these guns are worth using is yet to be seen. We are currently testing them and will update you as soon as possible. Tier One Modes were also added to the game. Tier One is essentially a realism mode for core 6 vs 6 multiplayer, you have no HUD and half your normal health which also means you die twice as fast. This game mode creates a more tactical style of gameplay as it forces you to be more attentive and careful. Finally, a very important change was the increments to the game's footstep audio. This makes wearing headphones and listening out for enemy footsteps an incredibly vital part of the gameplay.
