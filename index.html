Call of Duty: Gun Guide

Welcome to Gun Guide where we provide information on the best guns in Modern Warfare 2 and show you their ideal setups!

Top 5 Guns

  • 5. VEL 46 (SMG)

    • The VEL 46, AKA the MP7, has a potential TTK of 189ms which is competitive, but still slightly slower than some other SMGs. However, it does have a very high fire rate of 952 rpm as well as a 40 round magazine. This makes the VEL 46 an extremely forgiving gun and an excellent choice for casual players who are not as confident in their aim.


    Best Attachments

    While it is usually better to utilise all five attachment slots on your gun, the VEL 46 is notably most effective with only three attachments; The Commando Foregrip, the Slimline Pro Optic and the VLK LZR 7MW Laser.



  • 4. STB 556(Assault Rifle)

    • The STB 556 is excellent for aggressive players. Its incredibly fast Sprint to Fire speed of 110ms, 234ms when tactical sprinting, allows you to almost always come out on top in short to mid range fights. Not to mention, it has an incredibly fast TTK of 162ms.


    Best Attachments

    The ideal attachments for the STB 556 are the Sakin Thread-40 Muzzle, the FSS Sharkfin 90 Underbarrel, the FTAC C11 Riser Comb, the Slimline Pro Optic and the VLK LZR 7MW Laser.



  • 3. Kastov 762 (Assault Rifle)

    • The Kastov 762 is a high recoil gun with monstrous stopping power. Even at longer ranges, the Kastov 762 has a potential TTK of 100ms. With the right attachments to balance its recoil and handling, the Kastov 762 becomes the perfect gun for mid to long range fights.


    Best Attachments

    The ideal attachments for the Kastov 762 are the Kas-7 406MM Barrel, the FSS Sharkfin 90 Underbarrel, the Kastov-Rama Stock, the Slimline Pro Optic and the FSS OLE-V Laser.



  • 2. Lachmann Sub (SMG)

    • The Lachmann Sub, AKA the MP5, is a great all-rounded gun with the fastest TTK potential up close at 75ms. Additionally, it has great bullet velocity, allowing it to compete at longer ranges than you would expect for an SMG. Finally, with a superb ADS speed of 100ms, this gun is without a doubt the best gun for close range combat.


    Best Attachments

    The ideal attachments for the Lachmann Sub are the Lachmann TCG-10 Rear Grip, the FSS Sharkfin 90 Underbarrel, the FT Mobile Stock, the 40 Round Magazine and the VLK LZR 7MW Laser.



  • 1. Kastov 74u (Assault Rifle)

    • The Kastov 74u is number one on this list because it excels at everything. It has a potential TTK of 92ms, second only to the Lachmann Sub. Its Sprint to Fire speed is above average at 210ms, 310ms when tactical sprinting. With a solid ADS speed of 240ms and easy-to-control recoil, this gun is perfect to use at all ranges.


    Best Attachments

    The ideal attachments for the Kastov 74u are the Sakin Thread-40 Muzzle, the FSS Sharkfin 90 Underbarrel, the Spetsnaz S10 Stock, the Shortac 330MM Barrel and the Schlager PEQ Box IV.
