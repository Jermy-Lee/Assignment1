Call of Duty: Gun Guide

Welcome to Gun Guide where we provide information on the best guns in Modern Warfare 2 and show you their ideal setups!

Top 5 Guns

  • 5. VEL 46 (SMG)

    • The VEL 46, AKA the MP7, has a potential TTK of 189ms which is competitive, but still slightly slower than some other SMGs. However, it does have a very high fire rate of 952 rpm as well as a 40-round magazine. This makes the VEL 46 an extremely forgiving gun and an excellent choice for casual players who are not as confident in their aim.


    Best Attatchments

    While it is usually better to utilise all five attachment slots on your gun, the VEL 46 is notably most effective with only three attachments; The Commando Foregrip, the Slimline Pro Optic and the VLK LZR 7MW Laser.



  • 4. STB 556(Assault Rifle)

    • The STB 556 is excellent for aggressive players. Its incredibly fast Sprint to Fire speed of 83ms, 158ms when tactical sprinting, allows you to almost always come out on top in close range fights. Not to mention, it has an incredibly fast TTK of 162ms.


    Best Attatchments

    The ideal attachments for the STB 556 are the Sakin Thread-40 Muzzle, the FSS Sharkfin 90 Underbarrel, the FTAC C11 Riser Comb, the Slimline Pro Optic and the VLK LZR 7MW Laser.
