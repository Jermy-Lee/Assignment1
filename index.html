Top 5 Guns

  1. PDSW (SMG)

    2. The PDSW, AKA the P90, has a potential TTK of 198ms, which is noticeably slower than some other weapons in the game. However, it also has a very high fire rate of 909 rpm, meaning that unlike other weapons, your TTK doesn't increase dramatically if you miss a shot. This makes the PDSW an extremely forgiving gun and an excellent choice for casual players who are not as confident in their aim.
